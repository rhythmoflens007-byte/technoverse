EU CBAM Compliance

The EU just changed
the rules. Are you ready?

The Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) is live. Every product exported to the EU must now carry a verified carbon footprint — or face a carbon tax at the border.

🇪🇺
What CBAM Requires
Exporters must report the exact embedded carbon in every product shipped to Europe. No proof = carbon tax at EU prices (€50–€100/tonne). Mandatory from 2026 for steel, cement, aluminium, fertilisers, chemicals.
⚠️
Without Verified Proof
Your buyer in Germany pays the EU carbon tax on your behalf — and deducts it from your invoice. No audit trail means no dispute possible. Your competitor with proof wins the contract instead.
✅
How Carbon Ctrl Helps
Carbon Ctrl generates machine-level CO₂ data, verified by AI, hashed with SHA-256, and permanently recorded on Polygon blockchain. One click → verifiable carbon certificate your EU buyer can independently audit.

FAQ

Everything you need to know.

Got more questions? Talk to our team →

What hardware do I need?▾
An ESP32 microcontroller (₹300–500) with a current transformer clamp and DHT22 sensor. Total under ₹600. Firmware via OTA — zero programming required. One device per machine, all monitored from a single dashboard.
Can I monitor multiple machines at once?▾
Yes — this is a core feature. The dashboard shows a fleet view with all machines simultaneously. Each machine has independent LSTM prediction, Q-Learning RL control, and its own blockchain ledger. The Factory plan supports up to 20 machines across multiple zones.
Is the Polygon blockchain data actually public?▾
Yes. Every AI decision is submitted to Polygon Amoy testnet. Anyone — including EU customs or your buyer — can paste the transaction hash into Polygonscan and independently verify the CO₂ saved, timestamp, and machine ID.
Is this actually compliant with EU CBAM?▾
Our data uses India CEA 2023 official emission factors and is blockchain-verified. For formal CBAM reporting you'll need accredited verification, which our Enterprise plan supports via SEBI/ESG audit integration. The blockchain provides the immutable data layer.
Will the AI throttling affect production output?▾
No. The Q-Learning RL agent applies minimum throttle needed to prevent CO₂ spikes. In real-world testing, production loss is less than 0.3% while achieving 12–18% carbon reduction.
